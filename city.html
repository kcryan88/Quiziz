Kepong Baru is a small township located in Kepong, Kuala Lumpur. Situated in the north of Kuala Lumpur, Kepong Baru is surrounded by a mountain range. Kepong Baru enjoys good connectivity and has plenty of amenities.

In terms of transportation, Kepong Baru enjoys good, well maintained roads. It is easily accessible and well connected to its surrounding areas as well as to the other areas of Kuala Lumpur. Some of the major roads and highways that make Kepong Baru easily accessible are Jalan Lang Emas, Jalan Mergastua, Jalan Kepong Baru, Jalan Lingkaran Tengah 2, Lebuhraya Selayang â€“ Kepong, and Lebuhraya Damansara Puchong. Busses passing through the main roads are quite reliable and one can also call a taxi anytime. There are two KTM stations just a short drive away as well: the Kepong KTM Station and the Kepong Sentral KTM Station. Given that Kepong Baru enjoys good roads and good connectivity, it is a township suitable not only for those who are working in this area, but also other parts of Kuala Lumpur.

Other amenities are also aplenty here at Kepong Baru. Restaurants and eateries in Kepong Baru offer the community here a wide array of food choices at affordable prices. Among the restaurants here are Xigong Seafood Restaurant which serves up delicious seafood dishes, Shui Yuan Vegetarian Court Restaurant which caters to vegetarians, Yee Wen Thai Food which specializes in authentic Thai cuisine and Popo Steamboat Restaurant. There are also fast food chains such as Subway and famous restaurant chains such as Secret Recipe in Kepong Baru. Other amenities in the area include clinics, pharmacies and places of worship. Most of the major banks in Malaysia have a branch located in Kepong Baru.
